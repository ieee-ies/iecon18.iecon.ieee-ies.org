Keynote Speakers

Susan K (Kathy) Land
IEEE Fellow
Program Manager | Missile Defense Agency & 2018 IEEE Technical Activities Vice President

Title: Cyber Security Considerations for Today’s Industrial Control Systems

Abstract: Ms. Land will discuss cyber security considerations for today’s industrial control systems taking a look at the unique, and not so unique, cyber threats posed to Industrial Control Systems. Based on her background and knowledge, she will describe some of the typical mistakes that seen again and again – even with the best cyber prevention frameworks in place and will provide a series of recommendations for cyber risk prevention.

Brief Biography

Susan K.(Kathy) Land is a Program Manager for the Missile Defense Agency in Huntsville, AL. She served as the 2009 president of the IEEE Computer, two terms on the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Board of Directors, and two terms as a member of the IEEE-USA Board of Directors (2013, 2106). She is currently the 2017 Vice President Elect for IEEE Technical Activities. She has more than 30 years of industry experience in the application of software engineering methodologies, the management of information systems, and leadership of software development teams. Ms. Land is the author or co-author of a number of texts, papers, podcasts, webinars all supporting sound software engineering principles and practical application of software process methodologies.
