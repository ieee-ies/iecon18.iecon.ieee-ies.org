1. More info on registration

Full registration (only papers associated with full registration can be included in the program):

  • Up to 2 papers can be associated with one full registration.
  • IES member: $750/$850 (early/late)
  • IEEE (non-IES) member: $800/$900 (early/late)
  • Non-IEEE member: $900 / $1000 (early/late)

Extras:

  • Additional paper (3rd and 4th, not more than 4 are allowed with a single registration): $400 each
  • Extra page (up to 2 per paper): $100 each
  • Spouse/companion registration: $350 (access to lunches, welcome reception, and banquet)
  • Additional banquet ticket: $200
  • Additional welcome reception ticket: $100

Discounted registrations:

  • No papers can be associated with discounted registrations
  • Student (IEEE member): $400 (includes access to whole conference offerings including welcome reception and banquet).
  • Student (non-IEEE): $450 (includes access to whole conference offerings including welcome reception and banquet)
  • Life IEEE member: the same as for students

One-day registration:

  • $350 (no access to social functions others than lunch and coffee breaks)

Registration Items

  • Author Conference Registration includes: Presentation and publication of up to 2 manuscript/s, Conference Attendance, Copy of Proceedings, All Tea/Coffee, Daily Lunches, Welcome Reception, Attendance to all Tutorials and Workshops, Banquet.
  • Non Author Conference Registration includes: Conference Attendance, Copy of Proceedings, All Tea/Coffee, Daily Lunches, Welcome Reception, Attendance to all Tutorials and Workshops, Banquet
  • One Day Only Registration includes: Conference attendance on the selected day, Copy of Proceedings, All Tea/Coffee, Lunch and Attendance to all Tutorials and Workshops on the selected day.

 


2. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does each paper need a full registration or can I register several papers with one registration?

A: Each full registration covers up to 2 papers; another two can be added for $400 each.

 

Q: I am a student. Can I register my paper at discounted registration rate?

A: No. Per Conference policy, for an accepted paper to be included in Conference, it must be accompanied by a full registration. Discounted registrations can be used for attendance, but not for including of a paper in the conference.

 

Q: If a student accompanies one professor that has a full registration, will the student receive an invitation letter for visa purposes?

A: No. The student needs to register in order to receive a letter. Every registration is accompanied with VAL (Visa Assistance Letter). For VAL questions, please contact IECON 2018 Registrar at: ieconreg@ieee.org

Q: I have registered for the conference, but have not received a Visa Assistance Letter.

A: Please contact IECON 2018 Registrar at: ieconreg@ieee.org (please include Registration Confirmation #).

 

Q: I have not received a Visa Assistance Letter but would like to use it for my co-author or student.

A: No. VAL (Visa Assistance Letter) is not transferable as it is tied to a specific registration confirmation number. If you wish to cancel your registration, please contact IECON 2018 Registrar at: ieconreg@ieee.org

 

Q: I have received a Visa Assistance Letter. Can you add my co-authors names to it?

A: No. VAL (Visa Assistance Letter) is tied to a specific registration confirmation number and is issused only to a person that has registered for the conference. If you wish to cancel your registration, please contact IECON 2018 Registrar at: ieconreg@ieee.org

 

Q: What is the cancelation policy?

A: All refund/cancellation requests must be received by 7 September 2018. There will be an administrative fee of 50% deducted from each refund. Registrations are deemed non-refundable after 7 September 2018. If you wish to cancel your registration, please contact IECON 2018 Registrar at: ieconreg@ieee.org.

 

Q: What about the papers to be included in the "Students and Young Professionals forum"?

A: For questions related to S&YP, please contactProf. Marek Jasinski mja@isep.pw.edu.pl.
