Dear Authors,
Congratulations!

It is our pleasure to inform you that the below papers have been accepted for presentation at the IEEE IECON 2018 Conference, to be held in Washington, D.C., Oct. 21-23, 2018. You are cordially invited to attend the conference to present your paper.

Please log into the manuscript submission website and carefully check the comments of the reviewers on your paper, which are intended to help you to improve the paper for final publication.

IECON 2018 Conference Policy requires that at least one of the authors of your paper registers at full registration fee to allow for inclusion of the paper in the program. One full regular registration covers up to two papers. Up to two additional papers may be added to the same registration for a fee. The registration system can be accessed from the conference web site.

Note that for the manuscript to be included in the program and the proceedings of the conference and be sent to IEEEXplore, all of the following three conditions must be met before the deadline of September 7, 2018:

  1. Final paper version uploaded.
  2. Copyright transferred to IEEE. This step is enabled in the submission system AFTER you have uploaded the final version of your paper.
  3. The paper must be associated with a FULL registration of one of the authors.

You can find the instructions for preparing the final version of your paper here.

Congratulations again and we look forward to welcoming you at IECON 2018 in Washington D.C., USA!
